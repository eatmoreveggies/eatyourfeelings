Ingredients:

2Tbs Chia seeds
1/2 cup plant milk
1Tbs vanilla
1Tbs maple syrup
Fruit or nuts for toppings - optional

Directions:

Combine ingredients in a jar with a lid and shake, shake, shake.
Allow it to sit for 15 minutes and shake well again.
Let sit in the refrigerator overnight
Top with fruit or nuts before serving
